Small Act, Huge Impact

Langit Watch was first introduced to the public through a network of large mosques in Greater Malang, the An-Nur Mosque in Batu City, the Sabillah Mosque in Malang City, and the Gadang Mosque (near the border of Malang City and Regency).

The AAQMS mobile used by Langit Watch has been completed a continuous analysis for one week for each mosque to determine the type and highest levels of pollutants around the mosque.

While the AAQMS is analyzing, our Langit Protector also shares information through an open seminar with everyone.

Most pollutants are produced by cars and motorbikes that keep the engine running even in the parking area.
